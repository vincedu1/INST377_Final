About the Project

Many people, including myself, struggle with maintaining a consistent exercise and diet routine when attempting to lose weight. There are fitness apps in the market, however, they often require subscriptions, have too many features that may be hard to use/understand for the average person, or lack the ability to provide personalized recommendations for the user. My project seeks to create a simple yet convenient way to track exercise and monitor progress.

Key Features

Exercise Library

Browse hundreds of exercises with detailed instructions

Workout Logging

Track sets, reps, and weights for every exercise

Progress Tracking

Visualize your journey with charts

Goal Setting

Set and track your fitness goals

Fitness Tracker Page Breakdown

  • Home: Dashboard with stats and recent activity
  • Exercises & Log:Exercise library with options to seach by name or filter by muscle group to find exercises that match your goals. Click any exercise to view detailed instructions and images. Workout logging form to record your training sessions. Enter the exercise name, sets, reps, weight, and optional notes.
  • Progress & Goals: Visualize your workout progress over time and set new goals to reach.
  • About: Defines the project and tells people what to expect, and a little bit about your information problem

API Usage

  • GET /api/workouts: Retrieves all workout data from your Supabase database
  • POST /api/workouts: Saves new workout entries to the database
  • Wger API Integration: Three fetch calls to load exercises, muscle groups, and detailed exercise information from the Wger Exercise API
